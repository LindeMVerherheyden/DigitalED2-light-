Sir Arthur Conan Doyle - The Adventure of the Norwood Builder

Welcome to the digital edition (light) of Conan Doyle's The Adventure of the Norwood Builder, made as a project for the 2024 edition of the Antwerp Digital Humanities Summer School.


During the 2024 Digital Humanities Summer School, we received an intense introduction to useful digital tools, such as:

  • Handwritten Text Recognition tools Transcribus and eSkriptorium;
  • Mark-up language TEI-XML;
  • HTR Post-processing tool Axolotl;
  • Keystroke Logging with Inputlog;
  • The online platform GitHub;
  • Encoding tools such as XSL, XSLT, XPath;
  • and a thorough practice round of HTML encoding


    • The culmination of our intensive one-week course: A Digital Edition of HTR on the work of Arthur Conan Doyle's The Adventure of the Norwood Builder.
